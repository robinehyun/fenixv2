FENIX RISE Training ‘17

WHY FENIX RISE--

As former basketball student-athletes and current basketball development professionals, we’ve noticed a necessity for alternative sports-based youth development programming, relevant to the modern needs of student-athletes.


FENIX RISE PROGRAM--

If there was a way to make the MIND - BODY - SKILL info each clickable squares lined up horizontally that lead to the pop-up screen for each that you showed me, that would be awesome. There’s hella content taking up this page, what do you think?

  • MIND: We’ve combined team-building exercises, self-reflection activities, and mindfulness tools to help student-athletes become calm, confident, and conscious decision-makers during live performance – we believe is the foundation to building a positive athlete and overall person.
  • BODY: Our goal is to build physically confident student-athletes who understand the importance of patience and persistence in life’s process. Through strength, explosive, and functional training, student-athletes are able to consistently perform at a competitive level, while remaining healthy during the long basketball journey.
  • SKILL: High-quality repetitions form high-quality habits. Using drills and techniques that simulate game-like situations experienced at the collegiate and professional level, we’re opening opportunities for our athletes to develop high IQ instincts and game-changing confidence.


FENIX RISE SESSIONS--

Each training session is 90 minutes, and a combination of either:

SKILL (60 min) + MIND (30 min)


-or-


SKILL (60 min) + BODY (30 min)
